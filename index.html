It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks sells hot and cold drinks, coffee beans, salads, hot and cold sandwiches, sweet pastries, snacks, and items such as glasses and tumblers. Through its Starbucks Entertainment division and the Hear Music brand, the company also markets books, music, and movies. Many of the company's products are seasonal or specific to the area where the store stands. Starbucks ice cream and coffee are also sold in grocery stores.
